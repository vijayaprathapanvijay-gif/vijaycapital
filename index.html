About

A Mind Built for Markets

CS Vijayaprathapan K is the founder and proprietor of Vijayaprathapan Capital — a firm born from a conviction that exceptional financial counsel should not be the exclusive preserve of large institutions.

Qualified as a Company Secretary and experienced across equity markets, corporate structuring, and strategic financial planning, he brings a rare blend of regulatory rigour and investment acumen to every client engagement.

His approach is direct, research-driven, and deeply personalised — whether advising a startup founder on capital structure or helping an individual architect a multi-decade wealth plan.

Our Vision

To build Vijayaprathapan Capital into a globally recognised investment firm rooted in India's growth story — delivering Goldman-grade thinking with the intimacy of a trusted advisor.
